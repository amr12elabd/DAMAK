Our Product Portfolio

Eight specialised plant growth regulators and biostimulants — formulated for Egypt's climate, crops, and soil conditions. Every product comes with full technical support from our in-field agronomy team.

Plant Growth Regulator

Power Contanol

A broad-spectrum plant growth regulator that optimises hormonal balance during critical vegetative and reproductive stages. Power Contanol improves cell elongation, strengthens stem structure, and helps crops recover vigour after abiotic stress events such as heat or drought episodes.

Biostimulant

Macovit DRW

A water-soluble micronutrient and amino-acid complex that enhances nutrient uptake efficiency through both the leaf surface and root system. Macovit DRW supports rapid canopy green-up, improves chlorophyll density, and primes crops for peak photosynthetic activity during high-demand growth phases.

Structural Support

Brace Br

Formulated to reinforce cell-wall integrity and reduce lodging risk in tall-season crops. Brace Br promotes compact, sturdy stem architecture by moderating internodal elongation without suppressing overall canopy development — protecting yield potential in exposed field conditions.

Flowering Enhancer

Sinolide One

A targeted plant growth regulator that synchronises and intensifies flowering initiation. Sinolide One improves pollen viability, increases fruit-set rates, and significantly reduces flower drop caused by temperature fluctuations or transient nutrient imbalances during the critical bloom window.

Multi-Action PGR

Trimax

A triple-mode plant growth regulator combining auxin-type, cytokinin-type, and gibberellin-type activity in one balanced formulation. Trimax drives uniform cell division, lateral root development, and early fruit sizing — giving crops a competitive head-start from germination through to the critical establishment phase.

Multi-Hormone PGR
Application
Foliar / Soil Drench
Growth Stage

Growth Controller

Limitora

A precision growth-control agent that curbs excessive vegetative growth, redirecting the plant's metabolic energy toward reproductive organs. Limitora improves canopy light penetration, reduces pest and disease pressure in dense plantings, and improves the marketable yield-to-biomass ratio.

Root Stimulant

Extra Indole

An indole-based root-promoting formulation that stimulates the development of a dense, extensive lateral root system. Extra Indole accelerates transplant establishment, improves water and mineral absorption in developing root zones, and reduces transplant shock in seedlings and vegetative cuttings.
