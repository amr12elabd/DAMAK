Our Product Line

A complete portfolio of plant growth regulators, biostimulants, and crop-protection formulations — developed for Egypt's climate and crop profile.

Growth Regulator

Power Contanol

A broad-spectrum plant growth regulator that optimises hormonal balance during critical vegetative and reproductive stages. Power Contanol improves cell elongation, strengthens stem structure, and helps crops recover vigour after abiotic stress events such as heat or drought.

Best for:

Vegetative establishment, post-stress recovery; suitable for cereals, vegetables, and fruit trees.

Biostimulant

Macovit DRW

A water-soluble micronutrient and amino-acid complex that enhances nutrient uptake efficiency through the leaf and root. Macovit DRW supports rapid green-up, improves chlorophyll density, and primes crops for peak photosynthetic activity.

Best for:

Foliar application at tillering or branching; ideal for leafy vegetables, cotton, and legumes.

Structural Support

Brace Br

Formulated to reinforce cell-wall integrity and reduce lodging risk in tall-season crops. Brace Br promotes compact, sturdy stem architecture by moderating internodal elongation without suppressing overall canopy development.

Best for:

Pre-heading application in wheat, maize, and sorghum; useful in high-wind or high-nitrogen field conditions.

Flowering Enhancer

Sinolide One

A targeted plant growth regulator that synchronises and intensifies flowering initiation. Sinolide One improves pollen viability, increases fruit-set rates, and reduces flower drop caused by temperature fluctuations or nutrient imbalances.

Best for:

Pre-bloom and full-bloom stages in tomatoes, cucumbers, pepper, and stone fruits.

Multi-Action PGR

Trimax

A triple-mode plant growth regulator combining auxin-type, cytokinin-type, and gibberellin-type activity in one balanced formulation. Trimax drives uniform cell division, lateral root development, and early fruit sizing — giving crops a competitive head-start from germination onward.

Best for:

Early-season root establishment and fruit sizing; effective across a wide range of field and horticultural crops.

Growth Controller

Limitora

A precision growth-control agent that curbs excessive vegetative growth, redirecting the plant's metabolic energy toward reproductive organs. Limitora improves canopy light penetration, reduces pest and disease pressure in dense plantings, and enhances the marketable yield-to-biomass ratio.

Best for:

Managing over-vigorous canopy in grapes, citrus, and high-density vegetable tunnels.

Root Stimulant

Extra Indole

An indole-based root-promoting formulation that stimulates the development of a dense, extensive lateral root system. Extra Indole accelerates transplant establishment, improves water and mineral absorption, and reduces transplant shock in seedlings and cuttings.

Best for:

Transplanting and propagation; highly effective for strawberries, nursery stock, and root vegetables.

Premium Root PGR

Damak Indole

DAMAK's flagship indole-acetic-acid formulation, optimised for Egyptian soil conditions and water salinity levels. Damak Indole promotes deep, robust rooting, increases resistance to soil-borne stress, and supports long-season perennial crops through sustained auxin activity.

Best for:

Perennial fruit trees, long-season crops, and saline or sandy soils where root anchoring is critical.
