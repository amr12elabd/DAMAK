Rooted in Egypt's
Agricultural Future

Since 2011, DAMAK has been building the bridge between global agronomic science and the Egyptian farmer — one field at a time.

Our Purpose

🌱 Our Mission

To equip Egyptian farmers with cutting-edge plant growth technologies and the agronomic knowledge to apply them correctly — increasing yields, reducing unnecessary inputs, and building farms that are more resilient to climate and market pressures.

🌍 Our Vision

A self-sufficient agricultural Egypt where science, tradition, and sustainability converge to guarantee food security for future generations — and where every farmer has access to the same agronomic quality that large commercial operations take for granted.

What We Stand For

🤝

Farmer-First Partnership

We measure our success by our farmers' results — not by units shipped. Every product recommendation we make starts with understanding the individual farm.

🔬

Scientific Integrity

We don't overstate what our products can do. Our agronomists give accurate, evidence-based guidance — even when that means recommending a competitor's product.

🌿

Environmental Responsibility

Sustainable yield improvement — not maximum short-term output at the expense of soil health. We support practices that leave the land in better condition than we found it.

📖

Knowledge Transfer

Every farm visit, technical workshop, and product manual is designed to leave the farmer more capable than before — not more dependent on us.

Our Journey

From a single-focus import business to a multi-service agricultural development company.

Foundation

DAMAK Agricultural Development is established in Egypt with a founding mission to import and distribute high-quality plant growth regulators to Egyptian farmers who previously had limited access to these technologies.

2011
2013

First Product Portfolio

Launch of the first dedicated product line including Power Contanol and Extra Indole — marking DAMAK's transition from importer to active product developer for the Egyptian market.

Field Agronomy Programme

DAMAK establishes its in-house field agronomy team — agricultural engineers who work directly with farmers across governorates, providing on-site technical guidance alongside product supply.

2016
2018

Portfolio Expansion

Introduction of Macovit DRW, Brace Br, Sinolide One, and Trimax — expanding the portfolio from root and growth support into flowering enhancement and structural plant management.

Damak Indole Launch

Release of Damak Indole — the company's flagship root-stimulant formulation, specifically calibrated for Egypt's saline-soil and high-salinity irrigation conditions. Reaches 8 governorates in its first season.

2021
2024

Land Reclamation Services

DAMAK expands beyond plant inputs into full land reclamation, irrigation design, and post-harvest logistics — becoming a comprehensive agricultural development partner rather than a product supplier.
