Rooted in Egypt's
Agricultural Future

Since 2011, DAMAK has been building the bridge between global agronomic science and the Egyptian farmer — one field at a time.

Our Purpose

🌱 Our Mission

To equip Egyptian farmers with cutting-edge plant growth technologies and the agronomic knowledge to apply them correctly — increasing yields, reducing unnecessary inputs, and building farms that are more resilient to climate and market pressures.

🌍 Our Vision

A self-sufficient agricultural Egypt where science, tradition, and sustainability converge to guarantee food security for future generations — and where every farmer has access to the same agronomic quality that large commercial operations take for granted.

What We Stand For

🤝

Farmer-First Partnership

We measure our success by our farmers' results — not by units shipped. Every product recommendation starts with understanding the individual farm.

🔬

Scientific Integrity

We don't overstate what our products can do. Our agronomists give accurate, evidence-based guidance — even when that means recommending a competitor's product.

🌿

Environmental Responsibility

Sustainable yield improvement — not maximum short-term output at the expense of soil health. We support practices that leave the land in better condition than we found it.

📖

Knowledge Transfer

Every farm visit, technical workshop, and product manual is designed to leave the farmer more capable than before — not more dependent on us.

Our Journey

From a focused import business to a comprehensive agricultural development company.

Foundation

DAMAK Agricultural Development is established in Alexandria, Egypt, with a founding mission to import and distribute high-quality plant growth regulators to Egyptian farmers who previously had limited access to these technologies.

2011
2013

First Product Portfolio

Launch of DAMAK's first dedicated product line — marking the company's transition from importer to active product developer for the Egyptian market.

Field Agronomy Programme

DAMAK establishes its in-house field agronomy team — agricultural engineers who work directly with farmers across governorates, providing on-site technical guidance alongside product supply.

2016
2019

Portfolio Expansion

Significant expansion of the product range — adding soil conditioners, chelated micronutrients, seaweed biostimulants, and a broader suite of gibberellin formulations.

61-Product Catalogue

DAMAK's product range reaches 61 specialised solutions spanning plant growth regulators, biostimulants, micronutrients, soil conditioners, dormancy breakers, fertilizers, and pesticides.

2024
2026

Today

DAMAK continues growing as a comprehensive agricultural development partner for Egyptian farmers — active at national agricultural exhibitions and expanding its field support network.

Leadership

The people driving DAMAK's mission — in the office, at exhibitions, and in the field.

Wafik El-Abd

Chief Executive Officer & Founder

Wafik El-Abd founded DAMAK in 2011 with a clear vision: to give Egyptian farmers access to the same quality of plant growth science that large-scale global producers rely on as standard practice.

Under his leadership, DAMAK has grown from a focused import operation into a company with a 61-product catalogue, an active field agronomy programme, and a presence at Egypt's major agricultural exhibitions.

"The goal has always been the same: give Egyptian farmers access to the same quality of agronomic science that global producers take for granted."
